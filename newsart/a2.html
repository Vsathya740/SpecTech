Limited edition OnePlus Nord with sandstone back coming alongside 8T.

OnePlus has its signature sandstone finish since the very first phone from 2014 and the tradition has been carried over via cases with newer models. Now, the company is going back to its roots and bringing a sandstone back finish to the OnePlus Nord.
The brand’s official Twitter profile confirmed a limited edition version of the midranger is coming on October 14 alongside the OnePlus 8T.
Currently, the Nord is only offered in Blue Marble and Gray Onyx colors so the new sandstone version will be a welcome change. In addition to the new phones, we’re also expecting two new TWS earphones with the OnePlus Buds Z and Bullets Wireless Z Bass edition.
We’ll have all the details in our full coverage right after Wednesday’s event.
